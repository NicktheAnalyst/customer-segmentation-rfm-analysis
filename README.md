📊 Customer Segmentation using RFM Analysis (Kenya Retail Dataset)
📌 Project Overview

This project applies RFM (Recency, Frequency, Monetary) analysis to segment customers based on purchasing behavior using a realistic Kenyan retail dataset.

The goal is to identify:

High-value customers
Loyal and repeat buyers
At-risk and churned customers
Opportunities for targeted marketing
🎯 Business Problem

Retail businesses often struggle to:

Understand customer purchasing patterns
Identify their most valuable customers
Reduce churn and improve retention

This project solves that by using data-driven segmentation to support strategic decision-making.

📁 Dataset Description

The dataset contains 3,000 transactions with repeat customers, designed to reflect real-world retail behavior.

Features:
CustomerID – Unique identifier (repeat customers included)
OrderDate – Transaction date (2-year span)
City – Kenyan cities (e.g., Nairobi, Mombasa, Kisumu)
ProductCategory – Type of product purchased
Channel – Sales channel (Online, Retail Store, Mobile App)
OrderValue – Transaction amount
⚙️ Tools Used
Microsoft Excel 2013
Pivot Tables
Data Cleaning & Transformation
Statistical Functions (PERCENTILE)
🧠 Methodology
1. Data Preparation
Cleaned and structured transaction data
Created a snapshot date for recency calculation
2. RFM Metrics Calculation
Recency (R): Days since last purchase
Frequency (F): Number of purchases per customer
Monetary (M): Total spend per customer
3. RFM Scoring

Customers were scored on a scale of 1–5 using quintiles:

Recency → lower is better
Frequency → higher is better
Monetary → higher is better
4. Customer Segmentation

Customers were grouped into segments such as:

🏆 Champions – High value, frequent, recent buyers
🔁 Loyal Customers – Frequent purchasers
💰 Big Spenders – High monetary value
⚠️ At Risk – Previously active but inactive recently
❌ Lost Customers – Low engagement
📊 Key Insights
Nairobi has the highest concentration of high-value customers
Mobile App channel shows stronger customer retention
Electronics category drives the highest revenue
A significant segment of customers is at risk of churn
📈 Business Recommendations
🎯 Target At Risk customers with personalized offers
📱 Invest more in Mobile App engagement strategies
💡 Upsell frequent low spenders to increase revenue
🛍️ Focus marketing on high-performing categories
